I'm actively seeking Postdoc/Lecturer Positions. Please contact me if you feel our research interest overlaps. I have strong programming skills and am excited about tackling important, pressing, difficult problems via principled, elegant, practical solutions.


I am a PhD student at Monash University studying with Li. Li and John Grundy. My research field interests mainly lie in the field of Mobile Software Engineering (i.e., Mobile Security and quality assurance) and Intelligent Software Engineering (SE4AI, AI4SE), under which I am broadly interested in applying static code analysis, dynamic program testing, and natural language processing techniques to strengthen the security and reliability of software systems.

Xiaoyu's research has been published in top-tier conferences and journals including ICSE, ASE, TOSEM, ISSRE, MSR, IST.

As a Female in CS
As a female studying computer science, I am always committed to encourage more and more women involved in computer science or other STEM fields.
