ANU PhD项目介绍

For ANU students:

If you are thinking about starting a PhD under my supervision, contact me early! I am happy to talk with you about the potential research topics. We can start by working on a research project, which will help you decide future research direction.

Multiple student projects are available in my group. If you are an ANU student (bachelor, honor, master, MPhil) and are interested in any one of them, feel free to contact me.

For International students:

Please read carefully: https://cecc.anu.edu.au/study/phd-mphil

Interested students are welcome to send an email toXiaoyu.Sun1@anu.edu.auand provide the following information：

  • Intended start date (2025);
  • Valid IELTS/TOEFL;
  • Personal resume and academic transcripts (including undergraduate);
  • Research interests and project/research experience (if any);
  • Published papers or open-source GitHub projects (if any).
